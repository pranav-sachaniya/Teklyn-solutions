Fashion E-Commerce Dashboard

Reimagining the digital storefront and inventory management experience for a leading modern apparel brand.

Client

Modern Threads Inc.

Role

UI/UX Design, Front-End Development, Backend Architecture

Timeline

4 Months

Deliverables

Web App Design System

The Challenge

The client was struggling with a legacy e-commerce platform that suffered from high bounce rates and complex inventory management workflows. Their internal team found it incredibly difficult to update product listings, while customers were abandoning their shopping carts due to a clunky, multi-step checkout process.

Our goal was to completely overhaul both the customer-facing storefront and the internal administrative dashboard to drive conversions and reduce operational overhead.

The Solution

We designed a highly visual, streamlined shopping experience focused on high-quality product imagery and an intuitive filtering system. For the internal team, we built a React-based dashboard that connects directly to their ERP, allowing for real-time inventory updates and analytics tracking.

The Impact

Post-launch, the new platform saw a 45% increase in mobile conversions and a 30% reduction in cart abandonment. The internal team reported saving over 15 hours per week on inventory management tasks thanks to the streamlined dashboard.
